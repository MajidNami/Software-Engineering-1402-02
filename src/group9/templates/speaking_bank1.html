IELTS Speaking Question Bank

Part 1: Familiar Topics

In this section, you will be asked questions about familiar topics like your home and family. Your answers should be neither too short nor too long.

Question 1: Can you tell me about your hometown?

Example Answer: My hometown is a small city located in the north of my country. It is known for its beautiful parks and friendly community. There are several historic sites that attract tourists, and it's a very peaceful place to live.

Question 2: What kind of accommodation do you live in?

Example Answer: I live in an apartment in the city center. It's a two-bedroom apartment with a spacious living room and a small kitchen. The location is very convenient because it's close to my workplace and public transport.

Question 3: How many people are there in your family?

Example Answer: There are four people in my family: my parents, my younger sister, and myself. We are a close-knit family and enjoy spending time together, especially on weekends when we go out for family outings.

Question 4: Do you have a large family?

Example Answer: No, my family is quite small. Apart from my parents and sister, we don't have many relatives living nearby. However, we do keep in touch with our extended family through phone calls and social media.

Question 5: Do you prefer living in a house or an apartment?

Example Answer: I prefer living in an apartment because it is easier to maintain and usually comes with amenities like a gym and a swimming pool. Additionally, apartments are often located in more convenient areas, close to shops and public transportation.
